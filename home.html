How It Works

From application to settlement

01

Initiate Instrument

Apply for any trade instrument digitally — upload documents, set terms, and submit for bank review in minutes.

02

OCR & Document Verification

Uploaded documents are automatically scanned and verified using OCR, reducing discrepancy rates and manual review time.

03

Compliance & KYC Screening

Built-in AML screening and KYC checks run automatically against counterparty data and sanctions lists.

04

Bank Approval Workflow

Configurable multi-level approval routing with real-time status updates, comments, and amendment handling.

05

SWIFT Issuance & Settlement

Instruments are transmitted via SWIFT and settled with straight-through processing to core banking and ERP systems.

Security & Compliance

Built for regulated environments

🔐

Multi-Factor Authentication

Every login enforces MFA via OTP, authenticator apps, or hardware tokens — ensuring only authorized users access sensitive trade data.

📁

Full Audit Trails

Every action — from document uploads to approval decisions — is timestamped, logged, and immutably stored for regulatory review.

🛡️

Role-Based Access Control

Granular permissions per user, department, and instrument type ensure the right people access only what they need.

🔍

Sanctions Screening

Real-time screening against OFAC, UN, EU, and local sanctions lists for every counterparty and beneficiary in every transaction.

UCP 600 Compliant

Uniform Customs and Practice for Documentary Credits — the global standard governing Letters of Credit, fully embedded in all LC workflows.

URDG 758 Compliant

Uniform Rules for Demand Guarantees — all Bank Guarantee issuance and claim processing follows ICC URDG 758 guidelines.

ISO 27001 & PCI-DSS Certified

Information security management and payment card data handling meet international certification standards, ensuring enterprise-grade data protection.
